=======================
MongoDB CRUD Operations
=======================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

CRUD stands for *create*, *read*, *update*, and *delete*. These four
abstract operations provide the fundamental basis of all client
interaction with a database. *Create*, *update*, and *delete* are the
core data manipulation operations, while queries are *read*
operations.
