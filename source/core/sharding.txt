=================
Sharding Concepts
=================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

the components, configurations, and behaviors of MongoDB sharded
clusters. For an overview of sharding, see
:doc:`/core/sharding-introduction`. For documentation on the
administration of sharded clusters, see
:doc:`/administration/sharded-clusters`.
