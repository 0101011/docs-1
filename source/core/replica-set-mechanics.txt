=====================
Replica Set Mechanics
=====================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

The replication of data among replica-set members occur through the
synchronization of a special :term:`capped collection`, called the
:term:`oplog` (operations log), that logs the operations that modify
the data. For more information on details of the replication, see:
