=====================
Replica Set Tutorials
=====================

.. default-domain:: mongodb

The following pages provide procedures for deploying, configuring and
maintaining your :term:`replica sets <replica set>`. In general, replica
sets require very little administrative intervention. MongoDB automates
most tasks associated with database replication.
